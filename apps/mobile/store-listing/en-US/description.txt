Collect digital stamp cards at your favorite cafes and restaurants — no paper, no POS.

Flotory is a loyalty wallet for hospitality venues. Join a venue once with a QR code, then tap the NFC stand on every visit to collect stamps toward rewards.

WHAT GUESTS CAN DO
• Join venues via QR or in-app discovery
• Tap NFC stands at the counter to collect stamps
• Track progress on your wallet and home screen
• Slide to redeem when your reward is ready
• See nearby venues and campaigns

HOW IT WORKS
1. Scan the venue QR or open your invite link
2. Install Flotory and create a free account
3. On return visits, tap the NFC stand at the counter
4. Unlock rewards and slide to redeem in the app

Built for independent cafes, wine bars, bakeries, and restaurants. No staff scanner required.

Venue owners manage loyalty from flotory.com.

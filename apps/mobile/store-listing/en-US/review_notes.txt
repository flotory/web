Flotory is a customer loyalty wallet for hospitality venues (cafes, bakeries, wine bars).

After sign-in, the demo account has stamp cards and may have a reward ready to redeem at Demo Cafe.

NFC
The app reads NFC tags programmed with Flotory tap URLs at participating venue counters. NFC requires a physical iPhone (not Simulator). Reviewers without a tag can open this deep link after sign-in to simulate a tap (same API as a physical stand):
flotory://t/democafenfcstandlocaltest00001

Venue join bridge (web): https://flotory.com/v/demo-cafe

No special hardware beyond a standard NFC-capable iPhone is required.

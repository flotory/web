Flotory is a customer loyalty wallet for hospitality venues (cafes, bakeries, wine bars).

GUEST BROWSING (no account required)
Open the app → Venues tab lists published venues without signing in. Registration is only required for loyalty features (join, stamp, redeem).

SIGN IN OPTIONS
- Sign in with Apple
- Continue with Google
- Email and password

TEST ACCOUNT (production)
Email: customer@example.com

After sign-in, the demo account has stamp cards and may have a reward ready to redeem at Demo Cafe.

ACCOUNT DELETION
Profile → Delete account (type DELETE to confirm). Password required for email accounts; OAuth accounts confirm with DELETE only.

NFC DEMO VIDEO
See the demo video URL in App Review Information (physical iPhone + programmed NFC tag).

NFC
The app reads NFC tags programmed with Flotory tap URLs at participating venue counters. NFC requires a physical iPhone (not Simulator). Reviewers without a tag can open this deep link after sign-in to simulate a tap (same API as a physical stand):
flotory://t/democafenfcstandlocaltest00001

## 3. Roles Under GDPR

For customer accounts, platform operation, app analytics, security, notifications, and user relationship management, Flotory generally acts as the data controller.

For certain venue dashboard features, Flotory may process data to provide services to venues. However, customers remain users of the Flotory platform. Venues do not gain ownership of customer personal data.

Venues may access customer information only as necessary to operate loyalty programs, honor rewards, prevent fraud, manage campaigns, and view analytics provided through Flotory.

Where venues process personal data outside Flotory or decide independently how to use personal data, they may act as separate controllers and must comply with their own legal obligations.
